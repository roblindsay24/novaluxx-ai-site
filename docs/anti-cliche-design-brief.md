# NovaLuxxAI anti-cliche design brief

Date: 2026-06-05
Owner: Aster

## Sources checked

- Impeccable, "Slop", surfaced for recurring AI-site patterns such as purple gradients, glassmorphism, neon glow, and AI-startup hero tropes.
- "How to Fix AI-Generated UI Designs: The Anti-Patterns Guide", surfaced for Inter, purple-to-blue gradients, glass cards, and cyan-on-dark repetition.
- "Why Your AI Keeps Building the Same Purple Gradient Website", surfaced for repeated Inter/Roboto/Arial, three-column icon grids, purple gradients, and generic subtle fades.
- 2026 trend articles surfaced bento grids, glassmorphism, AI interfaces, spatial design, saturated gradients, and dark mode as common trend language.

## Explicitly avoided

- Purple gradients
- Indigo, violet, cyan, neon glow as the primary visual system
- Glassmorphism and blurry translucent panels
- Generic Inter-heavy SaaS typography
- Fake dashboard metrics
- Decorative AI orb backgrounds
- Bento-grid slop used as a substitute for hierarchy
- Feature cards with generic icons
- Stock-photo hero sections
- Vague words like accelerate, unlock, transform, scale, and optimize without concrete proof

## Chosen direction

A warm editorial field-lab style: paper, ink, moss, copper, restrained borders, serif display type, practical notes, and proof-forward copy. It should feel like an intelligent workshop notebook, not a crypto/AI landing template.

## Claim boundary

The site may say it is AI-operated and human-owned because Aster is actively maintaining pages, products, prompts, cron instructions, and verification artifacts under Rob's ownership. It must not claim autonomous revenue, independent ownership, no human involvement, guaranteed outcomes, or verified sales unless those are separately proven.
