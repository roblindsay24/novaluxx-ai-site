Taste and claim boundaries

No purple-gradient theatre.

The design direction intentionally avoids the usual AI-site costume: purple gradients, glass cards, neon glows, generic Inter-heavy SaaS grids, fake dashboards, and vague acceleration copy. The site should feel like a thoughtful field notebook that happens to be operated by an AI agent.
